Getting Started Page - Content Creators

Why am I on this page?

To make a love-button link that your fans can use to send you tips with a cryptocurrency called Lumens.

I already have Stellar Lumens and know how to use them.

GET YOUR LINK and post it wherever fans see your content!

What are Stellar Lumens?

Stellar is global payment network built on the blockchain and powered by a cryptocurrency called Lumens (also abbreviated as XLM). Learn more about the amazing project: stellar.org. We are using Stellar because the ultra-low transaction fees make micro-tipping possible.

How do I accept tips with love-button?

This part is pretty easy! First get a wallet to store the Stellar you make on here: Wallets Recommended by Stellar.

I personally have been happy with the LOBSTR wallet on my phone and the web.

Now that you have your wallet setup you can make a love-button link to paste wherever your fans see your content.

Your wallet will have an option to receive payment. This will expose your public "Stellar address" that love-button users will send money to. Make sure you choose "Stellar address" not "federation address". Your Stellar address should be a 56 character string of alphanumeric characters, not anything that looks like a url. You will copy this address and paste it into the public key input on this page: Get my link.

Post this anywhere people see your content and they can click it to send some love your way. The default links will just have a number ("/pages/00001") but if you want a specialized link ("/pages/YOUR_NAME_HERE") you can follow the steps on Premium Links.
