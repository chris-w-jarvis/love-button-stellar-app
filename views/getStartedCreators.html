Why am I on this page?

To make a love-button link that your fans/supporters can use to send you tips/donations of any size with no transaction fees using a cryptocurrency called Stellar Lumens (from now on referred to as XLM).

I maintain an open-source software project

love-button aims to be the easiest way to support open-source projects with small non-recurring donations! Follow along with the steps at the bottom of this page and in no time you'll have a link to put in your project page that people who love your work can use to support it.

Why would I want this? There are lots of ways to monetize my content already!

love-button is a complement to all the existing ways you can monetize your content online, specifically targeting TWO distinct segments of your fans/supporters!

  • Group 1: The overlap of fans who will never turn off adblockers, never sign up for a paid subscription, and never buy your overpriced merch ;)
  • Group 2: People who stumble upon one specific thing you made, loved it, but are unlikely to engage with you more (ie become a full fan/follower).

For fans in the first group, being able to send small tips to support you without a commitment is ideal. Example of the second group: I want to learn how to code something, I search for it on Youtube, find a phenomenal 10-minute video that teaches me everything I was hoping to learn; I may never see their channel again but this video was more valuable to me than just "pressing like"! So I can send a tiny token of gratitude (a few cents even) to the creator with love-button!! And this few cents may be more than the creator would have got if I clicked an ad anyway. Due to high transaction fees, this would be infeasible with a credit card. I have been in both of these groups many times, so I decided to make this :D

What are Stellar Lumens?

Stellar is a global multi-currency payment network powered by a cryptocurrency called Lumens (XLM). It is one of the biggest cryptocurrency projects in the world. love-button is built on Stellar because its fast and the virtually non-existent fees make micro-transactions and micro-tipping feasible!

How do I accept tips/donations with love-button?

This part is very easy!! What you're going to do in this section is create a cryptocurrency wallet and then create a love-button link that sends money directly to that wallet! I recommend using Coinbase1 as your wallet because you can transfer XLM to your normal currency very easily.

Steps to setup your love-button:

  1. Download Coinbase app (or use web browser): Coinbase1. This will be your wallet!
  2. Make account, use a strong password and setup two-factor authentication.
  3. In Coinbase, go to "Accounts" view, scroll to "XLM Wallet" and open that.
  4. On the website, click "Recieve" in your "XLM Wallet". In the mobile app, in the top right corner of the screen in your "XLM Wallet", there are two icons. The arrow is to SEND XLM, the qr-code (square with pieces missing) is to RECIEVE XLM, click this one then click "I understand'."
  5. In GET MY LINK, "Wallet address" in your wallet (Coinbase) maps to "Wallet address" in the "Get my link" page and "XLM Memo id" maps to "XLM Memo id".
  6. Along with your wallet information (DOUBLE-CHECK THIS!!!!), add a name and description to personalize your love-button link. Press Submit!
  7. You're good to go, your link is live! Paste the generated link anywhere your fans engage with your content!
